DATA INSIGHTS

Revenue Distribution

* Revenue was broadly distributed across restaurants rather than concentrated among a small number of businesses.

Cuisine Data Quality

* Cuisine values often contained multiple categories within a single field (ex: North Indian, Chinese), requiring additional cleaning before deeper cuisine analysis.

Restaurant Activity

* Some restaurants existed in the restaurants dataset without corresponding order activity.

Project Development Process

* Several exploratory analyses were performed before narrowing the project down to the most meaningful business questions.

Key Takeaway

* Data analysis is an iterative process. Initial hypotheses were tested, validated, and adjusted based on the results of the analysis.
